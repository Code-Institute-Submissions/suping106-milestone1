Long Hill - Hidden Gem of North Shore

Long Hill - My favorite little park in North Shore!

So many wonderful surprises in one place...beautifully landscaped gardens, frog lily ponds, children's garden, cute chicken house, 20th centure southern style brick house, year around unique plants and flowers, low-key trails, Youth Food Project Farm. List goes on and on. Come and visit this amazing trustees' property.
